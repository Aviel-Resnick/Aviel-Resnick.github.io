Projects

  • Waffler. The Waffler is, in my opinion, the best of the many Wordle spinoffs. It claimed that every board was solvable in 10 moves, and therefore this project recreates the Waffle game with the purpose of determining the optimal solution to each daily Waffle. Thus this project verifies that each board is indeed solvable in 10 moves. It also includes the ability to play unlimited boards, restart games, and try out the official daily waffle without fear of failure. Visit the Waffle Game to see and play the official game. Credit to @thatwafflegame for the game concept.
